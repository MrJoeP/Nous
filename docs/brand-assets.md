## Character (LOCKED – Pencil Sketch Style)

**Style:** Pencil sketch, crosshatch shading, rough paper texture  
**Proportions:** Chibi – large head, compact body, short legs  
**Eyes:** Oversized, dark/empty, faint blue display glow  
**Clothing:** Black t-shirt, jeans, white Converse sneakers  
**Light:** Smartphone = ONLY light source, illuminating face from below  
**Background:** Nighttime urban street, skyscrapers  

### Three Variants

| Variant | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **CROWD** | Busy city street, pedestrians blurred |
| **SOLO** | Empty street, direct frontal perspective |
| **DARK** | Pure black background, maximum contrast |

## Brand Voice

- Direct, honest — no motivational phrases
- First-person perspective
- Short sentences
- Honest about setbacks
- Universally relatable — no name, no location
- **Core message: "No drama, no restart. Just keep going."**
